About DHAssist

Nobody likes to be uncertain about where and when lectures are occurring, and time used to find your class' seats is precious time wasted.


Even more so, nobody wants to be nagged at by lecturers for bring slow or late.


Developed by Goh Siau Wee of 5C33, this companion app aims to get students to settle down at lecture locations and get down with the lecture quicker.

For inquiries and feedback, contact the developer at goh.siauwee@dhs.sg.
